humongous story I
enchanta castle




enchanta castle was the biggest minecraft build for a long time. it was built
for a world called humongous. enchanta castle was made for the king
of enchanta. the castle was around 50 blocks high, and 100 wide. it set many records.
the first floor is a redstone hidden door. it opens up to a really cool room.
the room behind the door includes an ender chest, a shulker box, and a bed. the
lighting is a jack-o-lantern. you can go down a latter in front of the door to see
the redstone that makes the door work. there's a secret passage-way that leads to the
dungeons.

these are some veiws of enchanta castle. the country that it is in is called
enchanta and thats why it is called enchanta castle. as you can tell, humongous
is a creative world.
the second floor of enchanta castle is the grand hall. it is ten blocks high.
it has a throne at one end of it and hidden treasure chests by it. it has netherack
torches for lighting, and giant pillars. it is very cool.

the third floor of enchanta castle is the defense floor. it has zombie and skeleton
spawners for combat training.

it also has the doors to the walls of enchanta.
the walls have a checkerboard of purple and blue carpet on them. the lighting for
the walls is glowstone.





humongous story II
the mansion
one of the biggest builds in humongous was the mansion. it is not as big as enchanta
castle, but it still is huge. it's made primarily of quartz and pur pur(the national
block of enchanta) it also includes daimond, gold, and end rods. there is a four-poster
bed on the second floor and cakes on the first. the main lighting for the first floor

there is also lava lighting for both floors. I made this mansion for edialophulus




stories of true survival

we will survive

we will survive was one of the best survival worlds ever. we had a big castle called
five-level fortress in a base called taiga tower. it had a farm, and a horse with
daimond horse armor. the horse was named spruce with a nametag that we got from a dungeon.
we also accidentally caused a raid on a taiga village and got killed four times in a row
by a vindicator. it was not fun. but eventually, the raid stopped. the village had
no iron golem.

life in taiga tower

life in taiga tower is not too difficult. we have lots of good stuff in taiga tower. like a
room made entirely out of obsidian. with an enchantment table, grindstone, and cauldron.
the worst place in taiga tower is the nether. we have a nice place 'n' all, but zombie pigmen
have put it under seige. we do have a bunch of glowstone though. we've started to build a
nether brick part of five-level fortress, but our supplie of netherack to smelt was cut short when
the zombie pigmen laid seige on our nether house.

we used to have like, five cats in taiga tower, but then we accidentally killed one, another
stepped into a netherack fire torch, another tried to swim in lava, and we were left with only
snowy, who decided to take a trip to the nether with us. thankfully, she survived, and we got
her back to the overworld, where we sat her.

most of our tools in we will survive are enchanted, because we got a bunch of lapis lazuli
from our mine. we once had an enchanted fishing rod called fishy rod, it was very sad
when it broke. we also had an enchanted daimond pickaxe called ultra pick. it was very fast
at mining. we also had an enchanted golden pickaxe called ultre. it was pretty fast but
couldn't mine ores.

we will survive again

we will survive again was a survival world we created not too long ago, in which we have an enchanted
daimond pickaxe called ultra pick II. we also have a giant under ground fortress
that starts in a ravine with a chicken coop on one of the edges. there is a big giant man made river running
through the ravine, and a redstone inescapable piston trap outside of the door. we have three beds in the
first room you see when you go in, the middle one is yellow, and the outsides are blue. then, you open a
door on the right blue bed, and then go through a short hallway to our mine storage room, and beyond that,
with no door separating it from the storage room, is the mineshaft.

thirty minute daimonds

we were in our mine, still making the descending staircase, around level fourteen, when we broke one block,
and found the ultimate prize behind it. daimonds! and, it was the maximum amount
that you could find in a single vein. eight! and, we were only in the first half hour of our world!
after we had mined all of the daimonds, we quickly went back up our staircase, to our crafting area, and crafted
a daimond pickaxe. after that, we started a branch mine at the level we had found the daimonds at, which was
two blocks above the lava level. we found a few more daimonds, and started making an emergency bunker, which was an
under ground room made entirely out of cobblestone. we found eight more daimonds in the process, making a new record
for most daimonds found. 22! after finding more of the daimonds, we made some more branch mines, and ended
up making a maze of underground tunnels. we found tons and tons of obsidian, and made a nether portal. as soon as we
got to the nether, we made a portal protecter in about thirty seconds, and went back to the overworld. we made a
chicken farm at the entrance to the ravine, and put hoppers in each corner. now, every time a chicken lays an egg,
the hoppers will collect it.
