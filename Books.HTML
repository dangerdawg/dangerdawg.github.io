book ratings.

E, everyone

g, most people. may contain traces of adult content.

pg, decide if you want to read or not. may have some ungood things.

13, scary, probably want to be around 13 to read the whole book

m, pretty scary, probably want parents to decide if you can read it. may have adult themes

r, parents should definetly decide if you can read it. has adult themes and/or extreme violence

r+, very violent/thematic/gory/sad. you should probably be of considerable age to read books with this rating

rr-, exremely thematic and violent and other stuff like that. rr, but a bit less (*_*)

rr, I don't think any books have this rating, but if they do, it is restricted for adult reading.
